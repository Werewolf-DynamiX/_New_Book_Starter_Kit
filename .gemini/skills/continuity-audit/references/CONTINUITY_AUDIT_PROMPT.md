## Cross-Reference Checklist

When comparing tracked items:
1. Compare every mention of a character's physical description across ALL chapters they appear in
2. Verify object locations are consistent (an object can't be in two places at once)
3. Check that location descriptions don't change between scenes (unless explicitly justified)
4. Do the timeline math: add up all "X days later" / "the next morning" references and verify they total correctly
5. Cross-reference ALL of the above against `context/FACTS_SHEET.md` — the FACTS_SHEET is canonical
