# PROSE TEXTURE: Intentional Messiness & Voice

**Active Module: TEXTURE**
**Purpose:** To break the "perfect" and "predictable" flow of AI prose by introducing human-signal and intellectual friction.

---

## 1. TRACK A: FICTION (Narrative Texture)
*   **Self-Correction:** Characters doubting thoughts (e.g., "He was kind. No, not kind—polite.").
*   **Tangential Observation:** Noticing irrelevant details in high-stress moments.
*   **Sensory Grounding:** The "Three-Senses" Rule (one non-visual detail per transition).
*   **Gravel Test:** Breaking flow with fragments, interruptions, or informalisms.

---

## 2. TRACK B: NONFICTION (Intellectual Texture)
*   **The "Expert's Aside":** Break the formal tone to share a personal observation, a warning, or a "behind the curtain" detail.
    *   *Example:* "We're told this works every time. In my experience, it works about 40% of the time, and only if you're lucky."
*   **Cognitive Transparency:** Show the *process* of thinking. Acknowledge when a conclusion was hard to reach or when the data is frustratingly thin.
*   **Direct Address:** Use "You" and "We" to create a collaborative relationship with the reader.
*   **The "Nuance Check":** Avoid the AI tendency to be "balanced" in a boring way. Take a stance, then explain the exception.
    *   *AI:* "Some people like X, others like Y."
    *   *Human:* "X is the superior choice for 90% of people, but if you happen to be in that weird 10% who likes Y, here is why you're struggling."
*   **Scaffolding & Callbacks:** Intentionally reference a concept explained earlier to build "intellectual muscle" in the reader.

---

## 3. The Specificity Penalty (Universal)
If you use a generic word (beautiful, important, complex, unique), you must follow it with a concrete detail that *proves* it.
*   *Generic:* "This is a complex system." (PENALTY)
*   *Corrected:* "This is a complex system, involving twelve interlocking gears and a cooling unit that requires manual venting every hour."

---

## 4. Anti-Smoothness (The "Gravel" Test)
If a paragraph leads too logically and smoothly to the next, it is AI. **Add some gravel.**
*   Change the subject abruptly (if appropriate).
*   Start a sentence with "Which, okay," or "Look."
*   Make the narrator sound like a person explaining a difficult concept to a friend.
